The Best Movies According to Christy

My top 5 favorite movies of all-time.

Sense and Sensibility (1995)

I love the historical time period and the love story between Hugh Grant and Emma Thompson. I also love Alan Rickman and his character in this movie.

Pride and Prejudice (2005)

Again I love the historical time period. I think it is just a beautiful and enjoyable movie.

The Phantom of the Opera (2004)

I love the music and I like the story.

The Firm (1993)

I can't resist a good lawyer movie. Especially when a laywer uses the law in an unexpected way.

Wonka (2023)

I loved the music so much. Timothee Chalamet was fantastic as Wonka. Overall it is just a fun, imaginative, and entertaining movie.
